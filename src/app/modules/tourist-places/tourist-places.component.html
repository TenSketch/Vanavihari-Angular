At a distance of 7 km from Maredumilli Bus Stand, Jalatharangini is a picturesque waterfall situated on the Rajahmundry-Bhadrachalam Highway in Maredumilli village, East Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. It is a seasonal waterfall and also one of the must-visit places in Maredumilli.

Jalatharangini Waterfalls is a cascade of running water amidst the lush forest and rocky terrain. This is a multi-tier waterfall. During monsoon season, this small waterfall is full of gush and would be a heaven for nature lovers. The water of the fall is crystal clear.

One needs to walk 150 m to reach the falls from the entry point, which takes 30 minutes. The trekking path is slippery and a little strenuous. The waterfall is maintained by local tribes, and they maintain the area by making steps and preparing an easy jungle path with side barricade supports. There is ample parking space for vehicles.
